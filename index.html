NYYS (New York Youth Symphony)
Tour in Spain!

Posted by: Eugene Hong

Date: October 20, 2018

It has been announced that on the 26th of June, the NYYS (New York Youth Symphony Members) and I will have a tour in Spain! I see this as an opportunity to get to know my fellow colleagues better and the culture outside of the United States. We will be touring around many populated cities in Spain such as Toledo, Es Escorial, Las Navas, Seville, Ronda, Malaga, and Valladolid. We will be staying in Spain for 12 days, so we have a lot of time to view the scenery, interact with the citizens, go to the beaches and more! I will be performing in all of the cities by playing with my chamber group as a pianist and in the orchestra as a violinist. The staff of the NYYS has put a lot of time and dedication to the trip; therefore, I will practice hard and try to make the most out of the trip!

Waldorf Music Essemble Concert

Posted by: Eugene Hong

Date: October 24, 2018

On November 8th, 2018, The Waldorf School of Garden City will be hosting its first music concert of the 2018-2019 school year! The pieces we will be performing are the Carl Friedrich Abel Keyboard Concerto in F major, WK 53 and the Serenade (Spanish Genre). I will be performing the Abel Concerto alongside with the Orchestra, and Jake Vu will be playing the Serenade as well. Dale Stuckenbruck, the conductor of the ensemble, will boldly lead the group to a successful and musical night for everyone! Come and listen!

Waldorf Holiday Assembly

Posted by: Eugene Hong

Date: December 14, 2018

On the 21st of December, the freshmen and junior class of the Waldorf School of Garden City will be performing an original song written by Charles Hammer called Chestnuts Roasting On An Open Fire. Alexios Kritas (Guitarist), Joshua Wang (Violinist) and I (Pianist) will be accompanying the choir. There will also be other performances from the other grades performing songs, acting out plays, and dancing. Come visit and celebrate and feel the holiday vibes before the huge two-week break from school! See you there!
